Advanced Loading Techniques

Whereas the basics of loading models and animations has already been
touched upon in the model-files and animated-models sections, Panda's
asset loading system is very advanced, supporting loads from arbitrary
sources, a virtual file system, and asynchronous (threaded) loading.
This section describes some of the possibilities in more detail.
